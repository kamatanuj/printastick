Our Story

A Journey of Precision
and Growth

Established in 1972 in Mumbai, La Prints & Poly Pvt. Ltd. began as a focused business house catering to public sector industries. Over the decades, we have evolved into Printastick®—a brand synonymous with reliability in the Indian stationery market.

Our growth is fueled by a commitment to in-house expertise. We don't just manufacture; we design and develop products that facilitate the day-to-day operations of India's most critical institutions, from major banks to government offices.

Today, our network spans the Indian Subcontinent, ensuring that whether you are a student in a college or a professional in a corporate office, a Printastick® product is never out of reach.

1972 — The Foundation

Started operations in Mumbai, focusing on public sector stationery.

1990s — National Expansion

Scaled to 4,000+ wholesalers and multi-state distribution.

Today — Printastick®

India's trusted brand for institutional and corporate stationery.
